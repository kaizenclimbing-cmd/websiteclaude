
## Ideas for What to Build Next

Here's an honest look at what's currently missing or underdeveloped across the site, roughly in order of business value:

---

### 1. Training Tips / Blog section
The Training Tips page currently just says "Coming soon." This is a real missed opportunity — it's a free SEO and trust-building tool. You could build a simple blog where Buster posts articles, tips, drills, and training insights. Articles would be stored in the database so you can add/edit them from the admin dashboard without needing to touch any code.

---

### 2. Admin reply composer (send email directly from the dashboard)
Right now the "Reply" button opens your email client via a `mailto:` link. You could build a proper compose panel inside the admin dashboard that lets you type and send a reply directly from the page — using the same Resend email system already set up. No switching apps.

---

### 3. Revenue chart — last 6 months
The Analytics tab shows stats for this month vs last month. Adding a bar chart showing the past 6 months of revenue would give you a better picture of growth over time. All the Stripe data is already available.

---

### 4. Admin notes on client submissions
When reviewing a consultation submission in the pipeline, there's no way to leave internal notes (e.g. "Called on 14th March, interested in Kaizen plan, follow up next week"). Adding a private admin notes field per submission would help track conversations and next steps without needing a separate tool.

---

### 5. Testimonials managed from the admin panel
The three testimonials on the homepage are currently hardcoded into the code. You could move them into the database so you can add, edit, and reorder them from the admin dashboard — no code changes needed to update your social proof.

---

These are roughly ordered by how much they'd improve day-to-day running of the business. Let me know which one interests you most and I'll build it.
